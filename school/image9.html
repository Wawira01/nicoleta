About...

K. H. Modi Kindergarten was founded in the year 1983, by the most trusted and philanthropic educational trust, The Ahmedabad Education Society.

Mission:

  • To awaken the joy in creative expression and knowledge
  • To light a lamp inside the human soul, and keep it going for a lifetime
Objectives:
  • To provide a rich environment conductive to thinking and learning by doing (experiencing)
  • To provide opportunities to develop language with proper articulation for better communication
  • To promote the child's physical development
  • To provide opportunities and experiences to promote a sense of responsibility
Co-Curricular Activities:
Music, Drama, Arts and clay modeling, Recitation, Sports and Drill and Pyramid, Quiz, YOGA, Exhibitions, Trips
